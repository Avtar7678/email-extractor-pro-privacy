Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Email, Phone & Social Lead Extractor v1.3.0

📌 Short version: We collect nothing. All processing happens locally in your browser. No data is ever sent to any server we operate, and we use no analytics or tracking.

1. What This Extension Does

This extension scans web pages to find and collect publicly available business-contact information — email addresses, phone numbers, and social-media profile links — to help users build their own lead lists. It operates in these modes:

  • Quick Scan — reads the content of the currently active tab.
  • Deep Scan — reads the current tab and fetches linked pages on the same site to look for contact details.
  • Bulk Scan — fetches a list of URLs provided by the user from an uploaded file and extracts contact details from each.
  • Maps — when you are on a Google Maps or Google Search results page, reads the publicly displayed business listings (name, category, rating, reviews, address, phone, website) so you can export them. This feature only reads what is already shown on the page you opened.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Specifically:

  • The extension reads web page content (HTML and visible text) solely to identify contact-information patterns (emails, phone numbers, social links, and publicly listed business details).
  • Any results found are displayed only within the extension popup and are never sent anywhere outside your browser.
  • Leads you choose to save are stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB and remain on your device only.
  • URL lists uploaded via the Bulk Scan feature are processed entirely within your browser's memory and are never uploaded to any server.
  • Exported CSV, JSON, TXT, and vCard files are saved directly to your local device using the browser's download mechanism.

3. Permissions Explained

  • activeTab — required to read the content of the page you are currently viewing when you initiate a scan.
  • scripting — required to execute the extraction script on the active tab's page.
  • Host permission (<all_urls>) — required so the Deep Scan and Bulk Scan features can fetch pages from any website you instruct it to scan, and so the Maps feature can read the Google page you have open. No data from these requests is stored or transmitted beyond your local browser session.

4. Third-Party Requests

During a Deep Scan or Bulk Scan, the extension makes direct HTTP requests to websites you instruct it to scan. These are standard web requests made from your browser — no intermediary server is involved. The extension does not load any third-party scripts, analytics, or tracking libraries, and does not sell or share any data.

5. Local Storage

Leads you explicitly save, and your in-popup preferences, are stored locally in your browser (IndexedDB and localStorage). This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time from within the extension.

6. Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted at this page with an updated date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the extension's support page or contact us via the Chrome Web Store listing.
